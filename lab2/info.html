5 Curiosities About Singapore

  • It's a City-State: Singapore is one of only three city-states in the world, along with Monaco and Vatican City.
  • World's Greenest City: Despite its modern skyline, Singapore is known as the "Garden City," with nearly 50% of its land covered by greenery.
  • Unique National Anthem: Singapore is the only country that has its national anthem printed on its currency, specifically on the back of the $1,000 note.
  • Banning of Chewing Gum: Chewing gum has been banned in Singapore since 1992, primarily to keep the city clean.
  • A Melting Pot of Cultures: Singapore is a multicultural nation with four official languages: English, Mandarin, Malay, and Tamil.
